What is CoboCoin?

Everyone is talking about it: HOW do we solve the "plastic-crisis" of the 21st century? Oceans, roads, deserts, and more are filled with it. Countries that contribute the most to the crisis are not able to design solutions and people don't have enough incentives to change the plastic mess. CoboCoin aims to fill this gap by allowing anyone to earn money in exchange for cleaning up our environment.

Cryptocurrency

CoboCoin is an ERC-20 token. Plastic bottles may be exchanged for CoboCoin and can further be cashed out to real money.

Anyone can join

In Mexico alone 82% own a mobile phone, while 46% utilize common financial services (debit, credit..). CoboCoin can be earned by anyone and is safely kept on our Ethereum blockchain. All you need is a phone.

Decentralized

Anyone can buy and sell. Companies, people, artists. We aim to make increase the utility of plastic.

Blockchain Technology

CoboCoin is a ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum platform. We utilize open source software and frameworks like Zeppelin, Truffle, Ganache and Solidity.


What technology did we utilize?

  • Ethereum is utilised for the decentrlised tokenisation (ERC-20) of environmental detritus. We provide a cryptocurrency with real value to provide incentives for citizens to recycle.
  • Truffle is the development framework for Ethereum. It enables us to compile blockchain contracts, inject them into our web app, and can run a test suit easily.
  • Solidity provides a fraud-proof smart contract system that safeguards individual citizens and recycling plants. The smart contract enables plausible and trusted transactions no matter who you are.
  • Ganache provides a local test-network for our blockchain implementation (together with Metamask).
  • Amazon Web Services is CobaCoin's cloud-solution that not only constitutes its backend infrastructure, but also our AI-enabled community support
